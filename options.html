Hyped News Amplifier

Hyped News Amplifier is a little extension for Hacker News and Reddit. It allows you to easily spot news with most points and comments, while preserving list order.

Points and comments relative weight
% points
% comments

Feel free to configure how much points and comments count with respect to each other. By default it is 50 / 50.

My personal choice is 20 / 80: it means that I want comments to count 4 times as much as points, thus making posts with many comments stand out quite more than those with many points.

Put either to 100 to take that one into account and ignore the other.

Save your changes and reload the page to apply them.
