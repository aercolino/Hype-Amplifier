Hyped News Amplifier

for Hacker News and Reddit.

Hyped News Amplifier helps you easily spot news with most points and comments.

Weights

% points / % comments

If you chose 20% / 80%, then comments would count 4 times as much as points.
That would make posts with many comments quite more relevant than those with many points.
