Hype-Amplifier

Hype-Amplifier inserts white space before news according to their value on the current page. News with more points and comments will appear on the left.

Relative weight of points and comments

If you have 20% points and 80% comments then points are worth 1/4 of comments, thus making posts with more comments more valuable than those with more points.
